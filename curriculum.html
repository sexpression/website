The Curriculum

At Sexpression, we can help your school meet the requirements of the new National RSE curriculum. Our resources are tailor-made to your school and lesson requirements, accessible to all students and LGBT+ inclusive. Our curriculum is inline with the Department of Education statutory guidance.
